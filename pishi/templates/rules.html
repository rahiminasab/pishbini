Your score for each prediction is based on the following simple rules:

  • {% include 'icons/royal.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Royal Badge has worth of 20 points. You deserve one when your prediction is exactly the same as match results. In case of penalties, you will get this badge if you have predicted the exact number of scores that the winner achieves in penalties.
  • {% include 'icons/full_house.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Full-House Badge has worth of 12 points. You deserve one when you had a correct prediction of goal difference. In case of penalties, you will get this badge only if you have predicted the winner correctly and also the goal difference of two teams in penalty scores.
  • {% include 'icons/straight.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Straight Badge has worth of 8 points. You deserve one when you correctly predicted the outcome of the game.
  • {% include 'icons/one_pair.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    One-Pair Badge has worth of 2 points.You deserve one when you just had a prediction for a match.

Your prediction may deserve one of the following exceptional badges:

  • {% include 'icons/oracle.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Oracle Badge has worth of 20 points.You deserve one when you had an extremely rare but true prediction.
  • {% include 'icons/nostradamus.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Nostradamus Badge has worth of 15 points.You deserve one when you had a very rare but true prediction.
  • {% include 'icons/trelawney.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Trelawney Badge has worth of 10 points.You deserve one when you had a rare but true prediction.

*For Fifa World cup, the score that you get for a match in round i will be multiplied by 2i. Round numbers start from 0.
