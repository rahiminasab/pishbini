Your score for each prediction is based on the following simple rules:

  • {% include 'icons/royal.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}

  • {% include 'icons/full_house.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Full-House Badge has worth of 12 points. You deserve one when you had a correct prediction of goal difference till 120 mins.
  • {% include 'icons/straight.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Straight Badge has worth of 8 points. You deserve one when you correctly predicted the outcome of the game till 120 mins.
  • {% include 'icons/one_pair.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    One-Pair Badge has worth of 2 points.You deserve one when you just had a prediction for a match.

  • {% include 'icons/oracle.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Oracle Badge has worth of 20 points. You deserve one when you had an extremely rare but true prediction.
  • {% include 'icons/nostradamus.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}

  • {% include 'icons/trelawney.html' with extras='fa-2x' %}
    Trelawney Badge has worth of 10 points. You deserve one when you had a rare but true prediction.
